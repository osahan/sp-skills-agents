## 2026-06-27 — Agent created from the chai-reel session
- **Brief:** Move SP toward video; first deliverable a chai "Six Chais, One Ritual"
  stills reel; then capture the workflow as a skill + this orchestrator agent.
- **Did:** Established the stills-as-video pipeline (sp-stills-reel skill). Settled
  the design language (full-bleed, one focus per beat, slow pacing, editorial
  caption lockup) and brand truth (Playfair Display 400 / Open Sans, copper
  `#9E5F24`, from the vault design doc).
- **Learned / Action (seed lessons):**
  - **Start free, measure, then subscribe.** Stills-as-video proves the format at
    $0 before committing to Veo/HeyGen monthly costs. Validate engagement first.
  - **The viewer's eye is the brief.** The single biggest quality lever was fixing
    competing focal points — guard this in every concept.
  - **Inputs are the bottleneck, not rendering.** Photos, music, and fonts must be
    supplied as files. Front-load asset collection in the concept step.
  - **One shoot → many posts.** A consistent photo set yields a master plus
    per-product spin-offs; plan content in batches.
- **Open ideas:** per-product spin-off automation; 1–2 signature music tracks for
  series cohesion; a weekly scheduled "draft 3 reels for approval" task once a
  photo backlog exists.
