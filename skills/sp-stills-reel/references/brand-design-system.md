# Spice Pilgrim — Design System (for social/video)

> Source of truth: `brain/spice-pilgrim/design/design.md` (extracted live from
> spicepilgrim.com — Shopify Dawn theme). This is the condensed, social-relevant
> subset. If the two ever disagree, the vault doc wins — re-sync from it.

## Type
- **Headings:** Playfair Display, serif, **weight 400 — never bold.** The serif
  carries the elegance; bolding it cheapens it.
- **Body / labels:** Open Sans (400, bold 700).
- Slight positive letter-spacing everywhere. Tracked small-caps read as the
  "overline" label voice (e.g. `N° 02 · SMOOTH`).

Do **not** introduce new accent colors. If a frame needs emphasis, it's copper.

## Aesthetic rules that carry into video
1. **Photography-led, full-bleed.** Real product jars, warm natural light,
   wood/linen/slate surfaces. The photo is the background, not a boxed element.
2. **Flat & square.** No drop shadows on UI-ish elements, no rounded "cards."
   (Text shadows for legibility over photos are fine and necessary.)
3. **Restraint.** One headline, one accent, generous space. The brand voice is
   "Cook, sip, and live with intention" — calm, crafted, unhurried.
4. **Freshness / purity / hand-craft** are the recurring proof points
   ("days, not years"; "one ingredient: the spice itself"; "stone-ground").
